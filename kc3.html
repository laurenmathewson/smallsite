ABOUT

Kelly Clarkson was born on April 24, 1982 (age 37) in Fort Worth, TX but now lives in Nashville, TN. After graduating high school in 2000 she moved to LA to begin he music career. Shortly after moving, her friend told her about a new singing competition show called American Idol. After being one of ten thousand contestants, Clarkson went on to being the first winner of American Idol.

After her success on American Idol, she went on to release 3 number one hits as well as many Top 10 singles including Since U Been Gone, Because of you, Stronger, My Life Would Suck Without You, and Breakaway.

Kelly Clarkson's debut album 'Thankful' was a commercial success, selling over 4.5 million copies worldwide. It sold over 2.7 million copies in the USA and received 2x platinum certification. Her second album 'Breakaway' is her most commercially successful album to date. It received 6x platinum certification in the USA and was also certified multi-platinum in many other countries like Australia, England, Ireland, Canada, and New Zealand.

Kelly has been nominated for 15 Grammy Awards and has won 3. In 2006, she was awarded "Best Pop Vocal Album" and "Best Female Pop Vocal Performance". In 2013, she was awarded "Best Pop Vocal Album".

Apart from her music career, she has appeared in films and television shows. She also wrote a children's book 'River Rose and the Magical Lullaby,' and has been involved with various charitable organizations. Kelly Clarkson voiced as “Leah” in The Star and voiced as “Moxy” in the new animated movie Ugly Dolls where she also released a hit song “Broken and Beautiful”.

Kelly Clarkson joined season 14 of NBC’s The Voice as a coach where she won with 15 year old Brynn Cartelli (the youngest to ever win the show). Clarkson also won season 15, where she coached Chevel Shepherd (16). Kelly remains a coach today during season 17, alongside Blake Shelton, John Legend and Gwen Stefani. Today, Kelly stars on her own talk show, The Kelly Clarkson Show.
